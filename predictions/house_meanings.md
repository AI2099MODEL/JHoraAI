# House Meanings & Vedic Astrological Significations

This document outlines the core meanings, physical significations (Karakas), and life areas governed by each of the twelve houses (Bhavas) in the Vedic and Krishnamurti Paddhati (KP) astrological systems.

---

* **Body** → Physical body, constitution
* **Health** → General health and vitality
* **Life** → Life path and existence
* **Beginning** → Start of any new phase
* **Initiative** → Taking action
* **Personality** → Nature and behaviour
* **Character** → Moral qualities
* **Confidence** → Self-belief
* **Recognition** → Personal recognition
* **Recovery** → Recovery from illness/problems
* **Success** → Personal success
* **Identity** → Individual identity
* **Appearance** → Physical appearance
* **Head** → Head and brain
* **Independent Action** → Acting on one's own
